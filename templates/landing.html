Our mission

Textbooks cost more than most students can afford. Good notes are trapped in WhatsApp groups. Internet is unreliable. ElimuLocal was built to fix all three — a free, offline-first platform where students share knowledge with each other, openly and without barriers.

How it works

01

Register for free

Create an account in under a minute. No email verification, no waiting. Just a username and password and you are in.

02

Upload your materials

Share your notes, past papers, textbook summaries or lecture videos. Tag them by course and university so others can find them easily.

03

Browse and download

Search by keyword, filter by university and category, sort by most helpful. Preview PDFs in the browser or download for offline reading.

About the project

Built from a real problem, for real students

ElimuLocal started as a three-month project to solve a problem felt by every Kenyan university student — the gap between what institutions provide and what students actually need to succeed. It is built entirely in Go, runs offline on campus WiFi, and is free to use forever.
